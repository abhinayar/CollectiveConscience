About This Project

Founded by Florrie Marshall, “A Collective Conscience” received the 2018 "Interdisciplinary Arts Award” at Yale University's Center for Collaborative Arts and Media.

The collaboration between Florrie Marshall (School of Music, ‘18) and Abhi Nayar (Computing in the Arts & Economics, ’18) explores the nexus between live musical performance and audience engagement by collecting real-time data through this app that reflects individual audience member’s interpretation of the music, and translates the data into an aggregate artistic on-screen visualization.

The visualization will mirror the collective experience of the audience.
